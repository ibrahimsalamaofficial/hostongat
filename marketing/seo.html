Deciding which social media platform to use for your business can be a difficult task – which is where we come in!

Ask yourself these questions to help you narrow down the field:

  • What demographics are you trying to reach? Different social media platforms have different user bases, so it’s important to select one that aligns with your target audience.

  • What kind of content are you looking to share? If you’re planning on sharing mostly visual content, then platforms like Instagram and Pinterest may be a good fit. If you’re looking to share mostly written content, then Facebook and Twitter may be better options.
